A small confession

None of these recipes are actually mine.

They are all adapted from One, a recipe book by Anna Jones.

These happen to be some of my favourite recipes, and I wanted to put them all in one place anyway, so I thought, why not just add them into a website which collates my favourites? These recipes are also so delicious, I need to share them with whoever is reading this.

As you can see, this website is quite similar to Anna Jones'. I like the minimalist look.
